## What Is an Incorrect Quotes Generator?

An **Incorrect Quotes Generator** is a tool that creates humorous, out-of-context quotes by placing fictional characters into scenarios they would never actually say or do in canon. These "incorrect quotes" imagine characters in funny, absurd, or relatable situations, creating memes that fans love to share and remix.

### Why They're Called "Incorrect"

The term "incorrect" refers to the fact that these quotes are:
- **Not from canon:** Characters never actually said these things
- **Out of character:** Quotes that don't match their personality
- **Absurd scenarios:** Situations that would never happen in the story
- **Humor-focused:** Designed to make fans laugh

## Types of Incorrect Quotes

### Dialogue Swaps

Characters saying things others would typically say:

**Example:**
> Character A: "I'm not saying I'm Wonder Woman, but have you ever seen me and Wonder Woman in the same room?"
> 
> Character B: "We literally share a room."

### Situation Comedy

Characters in absurd scenarios:

**Example:**
> Character A: "Why are you like this?"
> 
> Character B: "Years of practice and zero self-awareness."

### Relationship Dynamics

Exploring friendships, rivalries, or romances through humor:

**Example:**
> Character A: "I would die for you."
> 
> Character B: "That's a bit dramatic, but I appreciate the sentiment."

### Character Trait Exaggeration

Highlighting personality traits through humor:

**Example:**
> Character A: "I have a plan."
> 
> Character B: "Your last plan got us arrested."
> 
> Character A: "This one's different!"
> 
> Character B: "You said that last time too."
